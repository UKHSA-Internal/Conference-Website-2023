UKHSA Conference 2023: Technology

Over the two day conference the Technology department will be presenting flagship products, innovations with Business Partners, and exciting demonstrations from suppliers.

Make sure to attend all the talks at the Technology stand, and participate in the quiz. All entries who register will be entered in a prize draw.

Agenda for Events at the Technology Stand

Demos:

Running throughout the two days as part of the UKHSA commitment to inclusion we will be demoing the new accessibility features of Windows 11 laptops Alongside the Windows 11 we will also be running an interactive demo of Microsoft's new AI assistant feature Copilot. On the Tuesday and Wednesday afternoons we will having interactive demonstration of Microsoft’s mixed reality Hololens.
